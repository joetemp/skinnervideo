Backstory

My name is Joe Templeton and I do video production in Denver, CO. I saw CITRA perform at a concert last summer and their energy was so insane, I knew I had to shoot a video for them. Even if I had to do it for free. So, I reached out to the band to express my interest. I told them I had a concept for a video that took place in a school. They were also interested but we needed a school.

So, we reached out to several historic Denver schools, in search of a stunning location. Many schools were interested but one school in particular stood out: Skinner Middle School. It’s the perfect backdrop for our story and their Principal, Grant Varveris, was down with the project from day one. He has been extremely supportive in making this project a reality. We are planning to shoot a music video at the school, with the help of the students. We want them to be a part of our cast and crew! We also plan to monetize the project and give Skinner the proceeds. Maybe it will help them start a film/video program!
