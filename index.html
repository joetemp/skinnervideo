Video Shoot

On June 1st, 2nd, and 3rd, CITRA will be filming their new music video at Skinner Middle School. As part of the shoot, they will be performing 2 free concerts on June 2nd and June 3rd from 10AM - 2PM in the Skinner Auditorium. ALL Denver area middle school-aged kids are invited to attend! On June 1st however, ONLY the cast will be allowed inside the school.

If you are a cast member or the parent of one, please download a video shoot info sheet here.

Attention parents: We have a SignUp Genius page set up for those of you who would like to help with the shoot! Click here to see how you can help!

If you are NOT part of the cast, but would like to attend the FREE shows on Sunday, June 2nd and Monday, June 3rd, please do! They are open to ALL middle school-aged kids and start at 10 AM! Come early to get in line!

Skinner Middle School is located at 3435 W 40th Ave, Denver, CO 80211

We hope to see you there!

Cast

After holding auditions in the Skinner auditorium and seeing a LOT of great kids try out, we have chosen our cast for the music video! While ALL Denver area middle school kids are welcome to attend the free concerts on June 2nd and June 3rd, the cast will be featured in the video more prominantly and will film several additional scenes, outside the auditorium. They are also the only kids allowed on set June 1st.
